Goal

How do Harvard students choose which clubs to join?

Hearsay? Reputation? Food-filled information sessions that promise glory?

Our goal is to provide a comprehensive platform for students to provide feedback about and learn about clubs on campus, so that we can be better informed in our decision making process.

Create an account to get started and help provide constructive feedback and advice.
